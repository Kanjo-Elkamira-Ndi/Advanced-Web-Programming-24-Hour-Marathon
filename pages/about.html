About BookSwap

We're building a community where books find new readers and stories continue to inspire. Founded in 2020, BookSwap connects book lovers across neighborhoods, making it easy to share your favorite reads and discover new ones.

Our Mission

We believe that every book deserves to be read, shared, and loved. Our platform makes it simple for readers to exchange books with others in their community, reducing waste while building connections between fellow book enthusiasts.

Whether you're looking to declutter your shelves or discover your next great read, BookSwap provides a trusted space for readers to connect, share recommendations, and keep great stories circulating.
